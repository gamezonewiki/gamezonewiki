CODM World Championship 2021 Qualifier: Garena Roadmap

STAGE 1 (Solo Qualifier)

DURATION

There are three weekends to qualify:

  • Week 1: July 8 - July 11
  • Week 2: July 15 - July 18
  • Week 3: July 21 - July 24

The path to the national championship in Malaysia, Singapore, & the Philippines begins with three open online qualifiers. Each weekend from July 8 - July 24 (GMT+8), eligible players could play ranked matches to win Credits, exclusive Call of Duty: Mobile esports cosmetics, and qualify for Stage 2, which is the Team Qualifier.

 

RULES

  • For each of the three weekends, once you click REGISTER under the PARTICIPATE tab, the tournament will go live and the first ten MULTIPLAYER RANKED MATCHES you play will earn you tournament points.
  • Tournament points are based on individual wins and your current rank; the higher your rank the more points you receive for a win. If you reach 60 points within the first ten ranked matches in any weekend, you qualify on to the second stage.
  • Players can qualify on one, two, or all three weekends; it only takes one weekend to qualify.
  • However, players are encouraged to play 10 matches every week to get the exclusive Manta Ray - 2021 MVP character skin.
  • Points do not carry over from weekend to weekend.
  • Note that in Stage 1 and throughout the duration of the tournament, competitors must be playing on a mobile device without a controller or other haptic assistive device. Players may not play using any desktop emulator. We reserve the right to exclude or investigate any player deemed in violation of this.

 

STAGE 2 (Team Qualifier)

SCHEDULE

Team Registration Period: July 26 - August 8

Tournament(Team) Period: July 29 - August 8

 

RULES

Stage 2 will require you to form a team consisting of players who qualified through Stage 1.

All teams have to play & complete 30 Ranked matches during the event period. Each win will yield points based on the team's average player rank. From the leaderboard in Stage 2, the number of teams below will advance to Stage 3 (National Championship):-

  • Top 8 teams in the MY leaderboard advance to Malaysia Championship
  • Top 8 teams in the SG leaderboard advance to Singapore Championship
  • Top 16 teams in the PH leaderboard advance to Philippines Championship

Please take note that if a team fails to play 30 matches, the team will be eliminated from the leaderboard when the event period ends. Teams who completed 30 matches will receive the exclusive skin reward which is Merc 5 - 2021 MVP.

  • Top 192 teams in the Philippines leaderboard will win 1,000 CP for each player.
  • Top 64 teams in the Malaysia leaderboard will win 1,000 CP for each player.
  • Top 64 teams in the Singapore leaderboard will win 1,000 CP for each player.

 

Follow Garena Call of Duty Mobile Facebook & Instagram for more updates.

 

Stage 3 (National Championship)

SINGAPORE CHAMPIONSHIP

Schedule: Aug 13 - 15 Aug

Format: Double Elimination Best-of-5 (BO5)

Prize Pool: SGD 20,000

  • Champion: SGD 10,000
  • 1st Runner-up: SGD 5,000
  • 2nd Runner-up: SGD 3,000
  • 4th Place: SGD 2,000

Champion & First Runner-up in the Singapore Championship will advance to Stage 4 (Garena Regional Qualifier).

 

MALAYSIA CHAMPIONSHIP

Schedule: Aug 20 - Aug 22

Format: Double Elimination Best-of-5 (BO5)

Prize Pool: RM 60,000
